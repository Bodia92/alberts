The formery

Where community inspires smart minds

where aspiration meets common sense

The Formery provides the same community, engagement and tenant experience as the iconic 1 Albert Street, but on significantly more attractive terms. The same smiling Concierge, meeting areas and improved technology in a curated workspace that introduces a new neighbourhood, midtown.
The essence of 1 Albert Street is being captured at the top of Albert Street. Providing a more attractive price point, the Formery has the Alberts workplace strategy, including Private Suites, Bespoke “made to measure” and Traditional office spaces all with access to a new Alberts Members Club.

Formery West

Formery West, complete with spectacular barbour views, is linked to the nearby Aotea Station and is easily accessed from Albert Street.

The building mainly consists of 14 Private Suites with varying views. Suitable for up to 30 people.

Book a Tour

Formery east

Formery East is a stone’s throw from Aotea Station, 20 metres to be exact, and a short walk to Sky City.

The building consists of 3,500sqm of Bespoke or Traditional office space, some of which can be continuous floors.

  • Hybrid Working

    Did you know that 68% of global corporate businesses want hybrid-compatible workspaces yet many are concerned about the threat to company culture that the work from home model brings?

    Alberts provides a hybrid solution that ensures businesses have spaces to inspire and enable collaborative working as well as offering isolated areas for quiet focus. Your clients don't lose company culture when the team prefer working in the Club more than their homes.

    Our tried and true method at the Formery will allow corporate culture to thrive, supported not only by an exceptional midtown location that is easy to access but also by the inspirational Club spaces which create the foundations of a community, ideal for networking.

  • Midtown Location

    Auckland's new midtown neighbourhood is highly connected, making commutes a breeze. Due to open in 2024, Te Wai Horotiu (Aotea) Station will see 54,000 commuters an hour entering and leaving the city at peak times. In addition to cultural facilities, vast skyscrapers housing luxury residences and hotels, other exciting projects due for completion in the coming period will provide new retail, restaurants, cafes and bars which are creating a thriving midtown neighbourhood around Formery tenants.
